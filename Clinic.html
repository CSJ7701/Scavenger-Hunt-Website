Mental Health Resources

Clinic

The Coast Guard Academy Clinic offers a comprehensive range of health services intended to improve cadets' well-being. These include general checkups, cadet counseling (therapy), phyiscal therapy, and more. In addition to routine care, the clinic serves as one of the primary confidential venues for restricted reporting in sexual assault situations, alongside chaplains and Sexual Assault Response Coordinators.
