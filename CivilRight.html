Civil Rights Office

The Civil Rights Office within the Coast Guard plays a crucial role in upholding equality and fairness for both military personnel and civilians. They administer Coast Guard policies pertaining to equal employment opportunities, anti-harassment measures, and diversity and inclusion initiatives, ensuring adherence to legal standards and fostering a respectful environment. They serve as a vital resource and reporting office for individuals seeking resolution and support for discrimination and harassment incidents. Additionally, the Civil Rights Office organizes comprehensive training programs on civil rights for recruits and cadets, emphasizing the importance of awareness and respect within the Coast Guard community. Contact details like emails and phone numbers are displayed around Chase Hall and academic buildings, mainly around the heads. Walk-ins are also welcome at their offices located in the Work Life Building.
