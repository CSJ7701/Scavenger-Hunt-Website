Company Officers and Chiefs

Company Officers and Chiefs serve as invaluable resources for cadets, offering support and guidance for various matters, whether urgent or casual. They are dedicated advocates for the success of every cadet under their care. Feel free to reach out to them for any pressing concerns or simply to say hello. Engaging with them not only fosters a supportive environment, but also provides opportunities to hear insightful sea stories. Please note that Company Officers and Chiefs are mandated reporters for any issue that could potentially cause harm to yourself or others.
